# SonoForge

A closed-loop **Design–Build–Test–Learn** optimization backbone for de novo design of the molecular biosensors and actuators that couple neurons to **ultrasound** — the enabling molecules for non-invasive, molecular brain–computer interfaces.

Flagship target: **gas-vesicle acoustic reporter genes (ARGs)**.
